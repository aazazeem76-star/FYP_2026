Process

How It Works

From registration to attendance in 3 seamless steps — powered by AI, secured by biometrics.

1
Register & Enroll

Create your account and capture multiple face samples from different angles. Our AI processes and stores your unique biometric signature securely — no photos are exposed, only encrypted embeddings.

  • OTP-verified email registration
  • Capture 10+ face samples automatically
  • Admin approval before access
One-time setup · ~2 minutes
2
Teacher Starts Live Session

The teacher logs in, selects the subject, and starts a live attendance session with one click. Students enrolled in that subject are instantly notified and the attendance window opens — no delays, no paper.

  • Teacher verifies identity via face scan
  • Session visible only to enrolled students
  • Stops automatically or manually
Real-time · Students notified instantly
3
Face Scan & Attendance Marked

Student opens the attendance page, selects the subject, and clicks Scan. A 5-second countdown gives time to position the face — then the AI runs 3 verification frames using Pearson correlation against stored samples. Match confirmed → attendance recorded instantly.

  • 5-second guided countdown
  • 3-frame multi-scan for accuracy
  • Proxy detection — only you can mark your attendance
Under 3 seconds · Zero proxy possible
All attendance records are encrypted, timestamped, and stored with full audit trails.
